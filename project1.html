Users can create multiple diaries to categorize their entries (e.g., personal, work, travel).
The ability to edit, update, and delete diary entries is essential.

The Diary Manager is a comprehensive digital solution designed to assist users in organizing
and managing their daily activities, thoughts and events. It serves as a virtual diary, providing
a centralized platform for users to record, plan, and reflect on their daily lives.

Robust search functionality to quickly locate specific entries or events. Filters for sorting entries based on date, tags, or other criteria.
